Darcy Price Strickland

18 March 1993 - 13 October 2022

Darcy lived freely, chasing the thrill of life one sunset at a time. Born and raised in the small suburb Flaxmere, he ventured big, crossing the oceans and capturing the world’s beauty across Asia, Australia and Europe. He made unforgettable impressions and memories with all those he met. He brought people together, and like the sun he beamed life, knowledge and love. His 6ft 4 height was overshadowed by his contagious,genuine smile. Art, music and sport enriched his time, and his abundance of talents enriched ours. He is someone to look up to, a role model, and our pride. This beautiful soul was created in a home of love, support, family and God. We will forever cherish your name and memory Darcy.

——Live life your way——
